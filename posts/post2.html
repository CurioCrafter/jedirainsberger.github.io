Tips for using Veo 3 effectively

Veo 3 is powerful, but there are some things to keep in mind when using it. Here are some tips from creators who’ve experimented with the model:

  • Budget your credits: Veo 3’s paid preview charges by the second. Start with a small number of renders to gauge your needs and scale up carefully.
  • Be patient and iterate: Generating the exact video you envision can take many attempts and hours of tweaking prompts.
  • Understand model capabilities: Veo’s different versions offer different modes. Veo 3 supports dialogue and audio, while earlier versions provide frame‑to‑video or ingredients‑to‑video.
  • Use AI to craft prompts: Tools like ChatGPT or Gemini can help you design more detailed prompts before generating your video.
  • Expect variability: Character consistency and unusual camera angles remain challenging. Some users report better results by keeping descriptions consistent and generating many versions.

These tips can help you save time and resources while producing better Veo 3 videos. Keep experimenting and share your findings!
