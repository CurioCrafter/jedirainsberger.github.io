Getting started with Veo 3

Veo 3 is a generative video model with audio support. It produces high‑fidelity video outputs and native audio such as dialogue and sound effects. If you’re ready to try it, here are the basic steps:

  1. Sign up for Google AI Studio or Gemini API: Veo 3 is available to Paid Tier users in Google AI Studio and the Gemini API. Pricing starts at about $0.75 per second of video output.
  2. Select the Veo 3 model: Choose the “veo-3.0-generate-preview” model in the API or studio.
  3. Write a detailed prompt: Describe the scene, characters, and actions you want. Veo 3 captures intricate textures, lighting effects and realistic physics.
  4. Generate the video: Use the API to send your prompt and wait for the operation to complete.
  5. Iterate and refine: Because output can be unpredictable, you may need to tweak your prompt and run multiple generations to achieve the desired result.

Once you’ve mastered the basics, experiment with different prompts and story ideas. Veo 3 makes it possible to create cinematic narratives and dynamic animations with synchronized audio.
